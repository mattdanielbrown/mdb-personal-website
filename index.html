I'm a 23-year-old artist, programmer, writer, and musician. I'm obsessed with learning new things and exploring creative outlets.

I'm fascinated by human-techology interactions and strive to make the world a more beautiful, effecient place. I love painting, writing/recording music (I've played guitar for nearly 15 years), and using art and code to build digital experiences that make people smile.

I recorded, produced, and scored the comedy album Dignity Optional, by Vic Shuttee and Tommy Waas, Available on iTunes.
